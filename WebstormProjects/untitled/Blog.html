Jon Snow

Though presented as the bastard son of Lord Eddard Stark, Jon is the son of Lady Lyanna Stark, Eddard's sister, with Rhaegar Targaryen. From infancy, Jon is raised by Eddard as his own alongside his lawful children at Winterfell and Jon's true parentage is kept secret. In order to escape his bastard status, Jon joins the Night's Watch and is chosen as Lord Commander Jeor Mormont's personal steward, and wields the House Mormont ancestral Valyrian steel sword, Longclaw, a gift from Mormont, who came to see Jon as a surrogate son.

Naruto

Naruto (ナルト?) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, an adolescent ninja who constantly searches for recognition and dreams to become the Hokage, the ninja in his village who is acknowledged as the leader and the strongest of all. The series is based on a one-shot manga by Kishimoto that was published in the August 1997 issue of Akamaru Jump.
